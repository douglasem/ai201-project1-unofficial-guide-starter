Declining balance

The Declining Balance Account is a convenient alternative to meal plan memberships. The Declining Balance Account allows students to deposit funds at any time and for any amount. Please make sure you activate your mobile GATORONE before adding the funds. Your funds cannot be added unless there is an active card associated with your UFID. Any unused balance carries forward from semester to semester and from year to year until students graduate or leave school. At that point, a refund can be requested, subject to a $10.00 administrative fee. 
Minimum $15 required, $500 if using Financial Aid
